Korean Chicken Bites

Intro

Korean Chicken Bites is a dish I would often go to for lunch or dinner, it is easy to make and very flavourful. Usually, I would eat this with rice. The following recipe is the way how I personally like, feel free to modify it in any way. And if you do so, click on the feedback button and share with me your newly modified recipe.

Ingredients

  • 450 g of chicken breast; cut into chunks.
  • 25 g of salt and pepper
  • 25 g of somked paprika
  • Chilli flakes
  • 25 g of baking powder
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • 1 tsp of honey
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • 1 tsp of ginger; minced
  • 1 clove of garlic; minced
  • 2 servings of cooked rice

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken breast into cubes.
  2. Mix the chicken bites with the spices and vegetable oil very well, and let it marinate for at least 1 hour
  3. Then, coat the chicken in flour and baking powder
  4. Fry the chicken bites at a high temperature until thoroughly cooked;
  5. On a seperate frying pan, mix the liquid ingredients together, as well as the garlic and ginger, until the aroma of the garlic gets strong, then add the chicken until it's coated well
  6. Add the sesame seeds and other garnish (that's up to you) and serve it with rice
