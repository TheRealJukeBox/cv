Brownies

Intro

Brownies do not require a lot of ingredients and time to bake, in fact, you would need less than an hour to bake a delicious portion. The following recipe is the way how I personally like, feel free to modify it in any way. And if you do so, click on the feedback button and share with me your newly modified recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 50 g unsalted butter
  • 200 g dark chocolate; preferably 60-80% cocoa
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder.

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven for 10 minutes at 180°
  2. Melt the butter and the chocolate in a water bath.
  3. On a seperate bowl mix the eggs, vanilla extract and sugar very well.
  4. Add the molten chocolate to the eggs mixture and mix them very well.
  5. Mix the dry ingredients and pour them into the liquid mixture.
  6. Add the mixture into a baking pan. Don't forget to add a baking sheet to prevent the brownies from sticking. and bake for around 10 minutes. Time varies depending on the thickness of the brownies.
