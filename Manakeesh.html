Manakeesh

Introduction

Manakeesh is a Lebanese dish that consist mainly of cheese and Za'atar, and is one of my favourite side-dishes. The following recipe is the way how I personally like, feel free to modify it in any way. And if you do so, click on the feedback button and share with me your newly modified recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 Lebanese flatbread
  • 1 1/2 cup of shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 4 tbsp of fresh za'atar
  • 4 tsp of olive oil

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven for 10 minutes at 200°
  2. Place 2 flat breads on a baking tray. Make sure to place the baking sheet.
  3. Add half of the Mozzarella cheese on both breads and top them with half of the za'atar.
  4. Sprinkle 2 tsp of olive oil on both breads.
  5. Repeat steps 1-3 and add the flatbreads on top of each other.
  6. Place the flatbreads into the oven at 200° for about 10 miuntes or until the flatbreads look ready.
